# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

OpenBrain welcomes constructive participation from people with different backgrounds and experience levels. We commit to a respectful, harassment-free environment.

Expected behavior includes:

- being considerate in technical and editorial discussions;
- focusing criticism on ideas and changes, not people;
- respecting privacy and consent; and
- helping newcomers participate safely and productively.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, and repeated disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may restrict participation when behavior violates this Code of Conduct. Responses are chosen in proportion to context and impact.

## Reporting

Do not use a public issue to report harassment, privacy concerns, or security-sensitive matters. Use an available private reporting channel associated with the repository. Reports should include only the information needed to investigate safely.

## Scope

This Code applies to project spaces and to public behavior when it materially affects the community or the project.
